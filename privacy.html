I. INTRODUCTION

| King Dhorajiya / KD: The House of Scion ("The House", "we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. |
| This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit kdscion.com or place an order with us. It applies to all visitors, clients, and individuals who interact with The House through any channel — website, email, or direct communication. |
| By using our website or placing an order, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Policy. If you do not agree with any part of it, please do not use our services. |
| This Policy does not apply to third-party websites or services that may be linked from our site. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party platforms you visit. |

II. WHAT WE COLLECT

IDENTITY & CONTACT INFORMATION

| When you create an account or place an order, we collect your full name, email address, phone number (optional), and shipping address. This information is necessary to identify your account and fulfill your order. |

BODY MEASUREMENTS

| The creation of your custom garment requires your precise body measurements. This is the most personal category of data we handle. It is stored securely, used solely to craft your piece, and never shared beyond the direct craftsmen responsible for your order. |

ORDER & DESIGN INFORMATION

| We retain records of your design selections, fabric choices, and order history to fulfill your orders, resolve disputes, and provide you with continuity of service in future orders. |

PAYMENT INFORMATION

| Payments are processed securely by Stripe, a PCI-DSS Level 1 certified payment processor. The House does not store, process, or transmit your card number, CVV, or full payment credentials at any point. Stripe's privacy policy governs the handling of your payment data. |

TECHNICAL & USAGE DATA

| When you browse our website, we automatically collect certain technical information, including your IP address, browser type and version, device type, operating system, pages visited, time spent on pages, and referring URLs. This data is collected in aggregate via Google Analytics and is used solely for site performance and improvement. |

COMMUNICATIONS

| When you contact us by email or through our contact form, we retain the contents of your correspondence for the purpose of responding to your enquiry and maintaining a record of our interactions. |

III. HOW WE USE IT

ORDER FULFILLMENT

| Your identity information, measurements, design selections, and shipping address are used exclusively to create, produce, and deliver your garment. This is the primary and overriding purpose for which we collect personal data. |

ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT

| We use your information to manage your account, maintain your order history, and provide you with access to your past orders and measurements for future commissions. |

COMMUNICATION

| We use your email address to send order confirmations, production updates, dispatch notifications, and direct responses to your enquiries. We will only send marketing communications if you have explicitly opted in, and you may withdraw this consent at any time. |

SITE IMPROVEMENT

| Aggregated, anonymized usage data helps us understand how our community interacts with our website so we can improve the experience, fix issues, and develop new offerings. |

FRAUD PREVENTION & LEGAL COMPLIANCE

| We may use your information to detect and prevent fraud, enforce our terms, and comply with applicable legal obligations, including responding to lawful requests from government authorities. |

NO DATA SALE — A FOUNDING PRINCIPLE

| The House of Scion does not sell, rent, trade, or license your personal information to any third party for commercial purposes. This is not a legal formality — it is a founding commitment of The House. From Day One, your details remain between you and us. |

IV. INFORMATION SHARING

CRAFTSMEN & SUPPLY CHAIN

| To fulfill your order, your measurements and design specifications are shared with our craftsmen in Bharat only to the extent necessary to produce your garment. These individuals are bound by confidentiality and may not use your information for any other purpose. |

SERVICE PROVIDERS

| We engage trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our business. These include: |
  • Stripe — Payment processing
  • Google Analytics — Website analytics (anonymized data only)
  • Email service providers — Order and transactional communications
  • Hosting & cloud infrastructure — Secure data storage
| Each service provider is vetted, operates under data processing agreements, and is permitted to use your information only as directed by The House and as required to provide their specific service. |

LEGAL REQUIREMENTS

| We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, court order, or governmental authority, or if we believe in good faith that such disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of The House, our clients, or others. |

BUSINESS TRANSFERS

| In the unlikely event of a merger, acquisition, or transfer of all or a portion of our assets, your personal information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email prior to your information being transferred and becoming subject to a different privacy policy. |

V. COOKIES & TRACKING

WHAT ARE COOKIES

| Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow the site to remember your preferences and understand how you interact with it. |

COOKIES WE USE

| We use the following categories of cookies: |
  • Essential cookies — Required for the website to function. They enable core features such as the shopping experience and account access. These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies — Placed by Google Analytics to collect anonymized data on site usage (pages visited, session duration, device type). No personally identifiable information is collected.
  • Preference cookies — Used to remember your settings and preferences for future visits.

MANAGING COOKIES

| You may control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling analytics or preference cookies will not affect your ability to browse or place an order, but may limit some functionality. Instructions for managing cookies can be found in your browser's help documentation. |

DO NOT TRACK

| Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" signal. Our website does not currently respond to Do Not Track signals. However, our analytics implementation is designed to collect only anonymized, aggregate data that does not identify you as an individual. |

VI. YOUR RIGHTS

RIGHT TO ACCESS

| You have the right to request a copy of all personal data The House holds about you. We will provide this information within 5 business days of a verified request. |

RIGHT TO CORRECTION

| If any information we hold about you is inaccurate or incomplete, you have the right to request that we correct or update it. |

RIGHT TO DELETION

| You may request that we delete your account and all associated personal data at any time. Please note that we are required to retain records of completed transactions for a minimum period as mandated by applicable law, and deletion requests may not extend to this data. |

RIGHT TO OBJECT & RESTRICT

| You may object to or request restriction of the processing of your personal data in certain circumstances, including where you believe our processing is not lawful or where you contest the accuracy of the data. |

RIGHT TO DATA PORTABILITY

| Where technically feasible, you may request that we provide your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format, or that we transmit it directly to another controller. |

OPT-OUT OF MARKETING

| If you have opted into marketing communications, you may withdraw your consent at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email or by contacting us directly. Withdrawal of consent does not affect the lawfulness of any processing conducted prior to withdrawal. |

CALIFORNIA RESIDENTS (CCPA / CPRA)

| If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act and California Privacy Rights Act, including the right to know what personal information is collected, disclosed, or sold; the right to delete; the right to correct; and the right to opt-out of the sale or sharing of personal information. The House does not sell or share personal information. To exercise your rights, contact us at communicate@kdscion.com. |

DATA RETENTION

| We retain your account and order information for as long as your account is active, and for 3 years following your last order, after which it is securely and permanently deleted. Body measurements are deleted upon account deletion. |

HOW TO EXERCISE YOUR RIGHTS

| To exercise any of the above rights, contact us at communicate@kdscion.com. We may ask you to verify your identity before processing your request. We will respond within 10 business days. |

VII. DATA SECURITY

MEASURES WE TAKE

| The House implements administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. These measures include SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmitted between your browser and our servers, and access controls that restrict who within The House may view sensitive data. |

PAYMENT SECURITY

| All payment transactions are processed over encrypted connections by Stripe, which maintains PCI-DSS Level 1 compliance — the highest standard in payment security. The House never handles raw card data. |

LIMITATIONS

| No method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. In the event of a data breach that affects your rights and freedoms, we will notify you as required by applicable law. |

VIII. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

| The House of Scion is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us immediately at communicate@kdscion.com. |
| Upon learning that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take immediate steps to delete that information from our systems. |

IX. INTERNATIONAL TRANSFERS

| KD: The House of Scion is designed in the USA and crafted in Bharat (India). In fulfilling your order, your measurements and design specifications are transmitted to our craftsmen in India. This constitutes an international transfer of personal data. |
| We take appropriate steps to ensure that such transfers are conducted securely and that your information receives an adequate level of protection consistent with this Policy and applicable law. Our craftsmen are contractually bound to use your information solely for the purpose of producing your garment and to maintain confidentiality. |
| If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, you acknowledge that your data may be transferred to and processed in countries — including the United States and India — that may not offer the same level of data protection as your jurisdiction. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as standard contractual clauses. |

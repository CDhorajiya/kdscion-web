Made to Order

Every garment from The House of Scion is created exclusively for you — from the moment your order is confirmed, our craftsmen begin working on your piece. Nothing is held in stock. Nothing is mass-produced.

Production Timeline

Please allow 3 to 5 weeks from order confirmation for your garment to be crafted. This timeline reflects the care and precision each piece demands. Rush production is not available — quality cannot be hurried.

Order Confirmation

Production begins only after your order is fully confirmed, measurements are verified, and payment is complete. You will receive a confirmation to your registered email address.

International Shipping

The House of Scion ships worldwide. Shipping cost and estimated delivery time are calculated at checkout based on your location. Delivery typically takes 5 to 14 business days from dispatch, depending on destination and local postal service.

Dispatch Notification

Once your garment leaves our atelier, you will receive a dispatch notification with tracking details to your registered email address.

Customs & Duties

International orders may be subject to customs duties, taxes, or import fees levied by your country's customs authority. These charges are the responsibility of the recipient and are not included in our pricing or shipping fees. We recommend checking your local customs regulations before placing an order.
